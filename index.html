About Me

Exploring creativity through color and form

I am Elena Vasquez, a contemporary visual artist with over 10 years of experience in painting and mixed media. My work explores the intersection of emotion and materiality, using vibrant colors and diverse techniques to create pieces that speak to the human experience. Each artwork is a dialogue between concept and execution, where technique serves the narrative.
